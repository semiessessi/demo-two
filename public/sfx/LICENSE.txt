Sound effects in this folder
============================

explosion1.wav, explosion2.wav, explosion3.wav, engine.wav

These were synthesized from scratch for this project (procedural Web-Audio-style DSP —
noise/sine layers with envelopes; no third-party samples). They are dedicated to the
public domain under CC0 1.0 (https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

You may overwrite any of these with your own samples. The loader (src/sfx.js) prefers
.mp3, then .ogg, then .wav for each basename, so dropping in e.g. explosion1.mp3 will
take precedence over explosion1.wav automatically.

cannon.* is intentionally absent — the weapon-fire hook stays silent until you add one
(e.g. public/sfx/cannon.mp3).
